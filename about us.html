Our Journey

How Khurshid built a tech empire in Uzbekistan.

2019: The Spark

Khurshid identifies a gap in the Uzbekistan market for premium, smart home appliances and launches Tech House.

2021: Growth

Expanding from a small boutique to a full-scale online store, serving customers across Tashkent.

2024: Innovation

Tech House becomes the exclusive partner for leading global smart-tech brands in the region.

Today: The Future

Continuing to bring the world's most advanced household technology to every home in Uzbekistan.
